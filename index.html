About me

KABUUKA Godfrey - 13th - 05 - 1998

I'm Kabuuka Godfrey, a passionate and dedicated male model originally from Kampala, Uganda, and currently based in the vibrant city of Dubai UAE. With a unique blend of African heritage and international exposure, I bring a fresh and dynamic perspective to the modeling industry. Born and raised in Uganda's bustling capital, Kampala, I have always been captivated by the world of fashion, Fitness and modeling. My journey has taken me from the pearl of Africa to the cosmopolitan landscape of Dubai, where I continue to pursue my dream of making a mark in the modeling industry. I possess a diverse skill set, including, Posing and Expression, Fitness trained, Versatility and Adapting to various styles and concepts for different modeling assignments. Outside of my professional life, I enjoy fitness and health training, bowling, the art of coffee making exploring new fashion trends, and traveling. These activities keep me inspired and motivated, bringing balance to my life. Goals Looking ahead, I aim to continue growing and making a positive impact, embracing new challenges and opportunities. My goal is to leverage my unique background and experiences to bring diversity and innovation to the fashion world.
